P.G. Diploma In Piping Engineering

P.G. Diploma In Piping Engineering

  • Online Live
  • 45 Days
  • 8 Modules

Course Outline

  1. PLOT PLANS AND PLANT LAYOUT

    • ✔️ Codes and safety considerations.
    • ✔️ Control Point & Battery Limit.
    • ✔️ Piping line lists.
    • ✔️ Drum Piping, Pump Piping.
    • ✔️ Column Piping.
    • ✔️ Jacket Piping.
    • ✔️ Line tracing: Types (Electric/steam).
    • ✔️ Compressor Piping, Heat Exchanger Piping.

  2. PIPING DRAWINGS & LAYOUTS

    • ✔️ Plant layout specifications.
    • ✔️ Plot Plan Development.
    • ✔️ Equipment arrangement drawings.
    • ✔️ Preparation of Piping drawings for field instruments & valves etc.
    • ✔️ Piping Rack & Spacing, Drawing pipe in the rack etc.
    • ✔️ Piping G A Drawing.
    • ✔️ Tank & Vessel Layout Consideration.
    • ✔️ Pipe Isometrics Drawings.
    • ✔️ Plant Piping System & Transportation Pipelines.
    • ✔️ Piping Procedures and workflow.
    • ✔️ Unit operations and Unit processes.
    • ✔️ Process Flow Diagram-PFD.
    • ✔️ Piping & Instrument Diagrams (P&IDs).
    • ✔️ Review of PDS.
    • ✔️ Line Designation table.
    • ✔️ Introduction of Piping System & their limitation.
    • ✔️ Introduction of P&ID, PDS & PMS.
    • ✔️ Introduction of key Piping equipments used in projects.

  3. FUNDAMENTALS OF PIPES

    • ✔️ Definition & Application of Pipe.
    • ✔️ Difference Between Pipes & Tube.
    • ✔️ Pipe Designators-NPS, IPS, NB, Pipe Wall Thickness & Schedule, Pipe Weight, Length, Grades, Ends, Joining Methods, Method of Manufacture, Pipe Rating & Pipe Symbols.
    • ✔️ ASTM Specification of Pipes.
    • ✔️ Piping restraints.
    • ✔️ Pipe dimensions and pipe representation.

  4. ASME/API CODES & STANDARDS

    • ✔️ Introduction to B31.3 process piping codes.
    • ✔️ ASME standards for Common Piping Elements.
    • ✔️ Piping specifications.

  5. PIPING MATERIAL SPECIFICATION(PMS) & PIPE CLASS

    • ✔️ Material selection.
    • ✔️ Material properties.
    • ✔️ Classification of materials.
    • ✔️ Material specifications (ASTM).
    • ✔️ Common piping materials.
    • ✔️ Piping specifications.

  6. MECHANICAL VALVES

    • ✔️ Basics of Valves.
    • ✔️ Types of Valves.
    • ✔️ Operation & Function of Valves.
    • ✔️ Control Valve Manifold.
    • ✔️ Valves Trim, Defects & Operators.
    • ✔️ Valves Selection.
    • ✔️ Valve Data Sheet.

  7. PIPING OF HYDRAULICS & SIZING

    • ✔️ Flow rate, pipe sizing calculations.
    • ✔️ Typical Velocities for water Piping.
    • ✔️ Reynolds number- laminar/turbulent flow.
    • ✔️ Darcy weisbach, Hazen William, Colebrook White Equation.
    • ✔️ Moody Diagram.
    • ✔️ Minor Losses in Pipe Fitting & their Calculation using Nomographs and Chart.
    • ✔️ Pressure drop calculations, NPSH Calculations.

  8. EQUIPMENTS USED IN PROCESS PLANTS

    • ✔️ Process equipment [Reactors, Towers, Exchangers, Furnaces, Vessels, Columns.
    • ✔️ Mechanical equipment [Pumps, Compressors, Storage tanks]
    • ✔️ Equipment foundations and supports.
    • ✔️ Equipment sketches.
    • ✔️ Equipment drawings.
    • ✔️ Equipment Vendor Data.
    • ✔️ Equipment lists.
    • ✔️ Typical equipment specifications.
    • ✔️ Equipment Layout Terminology.
    • ✔️ Equipment nozzle specifications.
    • ✔️ Nozzle Orientation & Scheduling.
